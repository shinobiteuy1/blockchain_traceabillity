# Channel Design

## Purpose

This document defines blockchain channel strategy.

Channels provide:
- ledger isolation
- organization privacy
- transaction separation

---

# Main Objectives

The channel architecture must support:

- organization privacy
- shared traceability
- regulator access
- scalable governance

---

# Initial Channel Strategy

## Global Traceability Channel

Purpose:
- shared lineage visibility
- common product tracking
- cross-organization traceability

Participants:
- Feed Org
- Farm Org
- Slaughter Org
- Further Org
- Retail Org
- Regulator Org

---

# Private Data Collections

Potential usage:
- confidential pricing
- supplier contracts
- sensitive operational data

---

# Initial Design Direction

Prefer:
- minimal channel complexity
- shared lineage visibility
- private collections when possible

Avoid:
- excessive channel fragmentation
